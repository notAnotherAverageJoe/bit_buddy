If you sell more Bitcoins than you currently own, your total Bitcoin balance will become negative. This situation can occur in financial scenarios such as short selling. For example, if you own 5 Bitcoins and sell 10 Bitcoins, your Bitcoin balance would be -5. This means you owe 5 Bitcoins. In this case, while you have received cash from selling those extra 5 Bitcoins, you now have a debt in Bitcoins. So, your cash balance increases, but your Bitcoin balance is negative, indicating that you need to buy back those 5 Bitcoins to cover your debt.
